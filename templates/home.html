Optimize Your Journey, One Click at a Time

Meet, Transportima.

Transportima or Transportasi Optimal is an innovative vehicle recommendation platform for your journey in various cities/regencies of Central Java. Benefiting from the latest technology, we provide accurate recommendations for three main modes of transportation: motorcycle, car, and train. Easily find transportation options that suit your journey. Explore Central Java with confidence, Transportima is here to ensure your travel experience becomes smoother and more comfortable.
